Welcome to Neon Cluck!

This captivating game pays homage to the classic hit "Flappy Bird" while adding its own vibrant twist. In "Neon Cluck," players navigate a neon-colored bird through a series of obstacles, reminiscent of the iconic pipes from Flappy Bird. The mechanics are simple yet addictive: Enter to start the game then click your spacebar or up_arrow to make the bird flap its wings, allowing it to ascend and descend between the obstacles. Timing is crucial as each successful pass through the pipes earns points, while any collision spells game over. This is a infinite game meaning there is no winning condition. With its visually striking neon aesthetics and easy-to-understand gameplay, "Neon Cluck" promises hours of challenging fun for players of all ages. Are you ready to take flight and soar to new heights?

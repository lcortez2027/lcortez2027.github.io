TRANSFER STAGE

During the experiment, we were tasked to pour the contents of the zip lock bag into the stocking, and liquid flowing out of the stocking was evident. We have noticed that this represents the nutrients the body absorbs and uses. Hence, the liquid was directed into the tray, and this acts as the body according to our observations.

However, we noticed that there were still some solid food contents left inside the stocking. This mimmicks the waste products that cannot be absorbed by the body. When the “waste” solidified, it was transferred into a paper cup with a small hole at the bottom; this represents the large intestine. The other cup was used to push the waste through the small hole, and this simulated the act of removing the waste.
